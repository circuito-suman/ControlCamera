Camera Exposure Modes and Supported Settings

  • Auto Mode: All settings are automatically managed by the camera. Manual entries like exposure time or aperture are disabled.
  • Manual Mode: Full control of brightness, contrast, saturation, hue, sharpness, gamma, exposure, white balance, etc. is enabled.
  • Shutter Priority: You control shutter speed; other settings like aperture are set automatically. Exposure mode controls may be limited.
  • Aperture Priority: You control aperture; shutter speed and exposure may be auto-managed.

Note that some controls such as backlight compensation and power line frequency might always be available, but slider interaction is disabled depending on the current camera mode.

Please refer to your camera's documentation for exact supported features as hardware capabilities vary.
